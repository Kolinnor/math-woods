# Math Woods Trademark and Branding Policy

The Math Woods name, logo, domain, visual identity, and related brand assets are protected separately from the software
and educational content licenses.

The AGPL license for the software does not grant permission to use the Math Woods name, logo, domain, or visual identity
for unofficial forks, public services, apps, social accounts, courses, or databases.

Forks and modified public versions must use a clearly different name and branding. They must not suggest that they are
the official Math Woods project, endorsed by Math Woods, or operated by the Math Woods maintainers unless they have
written permission.

Accurate, non-confusing references such as "forked from Math Woods" or "compatible with Math Woods exports" are allowed
when they are truthful and do not imply endorsement.
