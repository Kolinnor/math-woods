# Contributing to Math Woods

Thank you for helping Math Woods grow.

## Licensing

By contributing to Math Woods, you agree that your contributions are distributed under the project's applicable license:

- Code contributions are licensed under GNU AGPL-3.0-or-later.
- Educational content contributions are licensed under CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 unless otherwise stated.

Educational content includes problems, solutions, explanations, notes, concept pages, playlists, and learning resources.

## Originality and permission

Contributors must submit only:

- Original content they wrote themselves.
- Public-domain content.
- Content they have permission to publish under the applicable Math Woods license.

Do not copy problem statements, solutions, explanations, books, paid problem sets, contest material, or website content
unless reuse is clearly permitted.

When adapting, translating, or reformulating material from another source, cite the source clearly and make the new
expression genuinely your own. If the source or permission status is uncertain, say so or do not publish the material.

## Review and moderation

Reports of copied or poorly sourced content should be reviewed. Questionable content may be hidden or removed during
review.

Use edit summaries, provenance notes, and discussion pages to make changes understandable to other contributors.
